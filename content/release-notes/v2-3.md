Platform release 2.3 shipped to all hubs the week of January 12, 2026. A
maintenance release: no new surface area, three fixes worth knowing about.

- **Mesh rejoin after power loss.** Battery-only sensors now rejoin the mesh
  within 30 seconds of the hub returning from a power cut, down from up to
  10 minutes. No configuration change required.
- **Sandbox determinism.** `vd_test_` keys previously produced slightly
  different simulated readings across runs; simulated data is now seeded per
  key, so CI assertions against sandbox readings are stable.
- **App history rendering.** Run-history entries older than 30 days
  occasionally rendered out of order in the app. Ordering is now consistent
  with the API, which was always correct.

No deprecations, no API changes, no action required.
