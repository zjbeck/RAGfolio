Platform release 2.4 shipped to all hubs the week of March 9, 2026. Hub
firmware 2.4.x installs automatically unless auto-update is disabled.

## Lighting automation (beta)

The LED bar gains programmable day cycles: segments with independent
intensity and spectrum, sunrise/sunset ramps up to 45 minutes, and program
validation that rejects overlapping segments at save time. During the beta,
lighting programs are app-only; API endpoints arrive with the GA release.

The bar now also reports its output as a synthetic light reading so lighting
participates in microclimate scoring. Physical light sensors override
synthetic values in the same zone.

## Seasonal drift

Lighting programs can drift their start and end times to mimic photoperiod
change at a configurable latitude, up to ±90 minutes over a year. Drift
adjusts timing only — never intensity or spectrum.

## Mesh improvements

- Powered sensors now relay for battery-only sensors, roughly doubling
  practical range in most layouts.
- Firmware updates stagger per zone, and actuators mid-run are never
  interrupted for an update.

## Smaller changes

- Reading history buckets now include the raw sample count per bucket.
- Sandbox (`vd_test_`) keys simulate reservoir events deterministically.
- The app's live tile updates within one second of a mesh event, down from
  three.

## Deprecations

None in this release.
